Awards and Recognition

California Higher Education Sustainabil­ity Conference (CHESC)

"In an effort to educate people about how to address climate change, student Sage Lenier, a fourth-year conservation and resource studies major, won an award for a curriculum she wrote, 'Zero Waste: Solutions for a Sustainable Future,' about the science of modern landfills, the history of “trash” and how we can all reduce personal waste, among other topics. The curriculum, which Lenier has taught for the past three semesters in a student-led DeCal course, is a resource that she says gives everyone a practical guide on how we can apply existing strategies to re­duce waste. 'Trash is a human invention,' she says. 'There are solutions. We just have to implement them.' Lenier’s long-term goal is to have her course be a regular part of the curriculum at all University of California campuses." UC Berkeley Takes Home 5 Higher Ed Sustainability Awards
